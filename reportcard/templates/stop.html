What the Colors Mean

Good Service

Good service is when buses are spaced apart, reducing wait times and keeping more closely to posted schedules.

Bunching

Bunching occurs when a bus arrives at a stop within 3 minutes or less of the previous bus. Bunching indicates poor service, leading to empty buses, and more irregular and unreliable service.
