Buses are the backbone 
of our neighborhood. 

Jersey City residents depend on buses to get to work, school, and back home. But its hard to evaluate the quality of bus service.

That's why Code4JC built this site to provide a one-stop shop for statistics and charts on actual bus performance, drawn from the same data that powers NJTransit's mobile apps.Our goal is inform residents, businesses, and elected officials about trouble spots in bus service.

This website focuses on The Heights, where the Riverview Neighborhood Association has organized a petition drive for better bus service. You can learn about current bus service and take action by signing the petition.
