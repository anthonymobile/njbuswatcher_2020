Buses are the backbone 
of our neighborhood. 

Jersey City residents depend on buses to get to work, school, and the homes of friends and loved ones.

That's why Code4JC is supporting neighborhood organizations in informing residents, businesses, and public officials about the opportunities and challenges in local bus service.

This website focuses on The Heights, where the Riverview Neighborhood Association has organized a petition drive to lobby for better bus service. You can learn about current bus service and take action by signing the petition.
